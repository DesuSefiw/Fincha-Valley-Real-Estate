About Us

Founded on 14/3/2016 Ethiopian Calendar by Mr. Tarekegn Legesse Motora, our company aims to address socio-economic and environmental challenges faced by the people of Fincha and surrounding communities. We focus on housing, transportation, agricultural exports, and community empowerment.

Our Objectives & Vision

Objectives

  • Empowering employees and local communities
  • Promoting cooperation and unity
  • Addressing socio-economic and environmental challenges
  • Supporting local farmers with supplies like onions, oil, and flour
  • Creating job opportunities for 30,000 youth and women

Vision

To transform Ethiopia by 2030 through socio-economic development, moral upliftment, and community empowerment, inspiring a self-sufficient and prosperous society.

Our Projects & Services

  • Housing development for members in Addis Ababa
  • Transportation of sugar products and by-products to Addis Ababa
  • Engagement in agricultural export sectors (fruits & more)
